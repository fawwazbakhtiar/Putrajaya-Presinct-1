DISCOVER THE PRESINT-1 IN PUTRAJAYA

Presint 1 in Putrajaya is known as the Government Presint and houses many key federal government buildings. It's located at the northern end of Putrajaya's core area and is a focal point for the city's administration. Key landmarks in Presint 1 include Perdana Putra (the Prime Minister's Office), Putra Square, and the Putra Mosque. The area also features Putra Perdana Park, which marks Putrajaya's establishment and houses a time capsule charting the city's history.


Putrajaya Presint-1 is also comes with various other facilities and activities such as Hotel, Food Stalls, Mosque, Lakes, Parks, Cruise Tours, Beam Scooter, Cycling, Jogging, and many more.
