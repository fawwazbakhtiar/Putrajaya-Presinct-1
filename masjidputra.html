Putra Mosque (Pink Mosque)

The most iconic religious landmark in Putrajaya, known for its pink dome and lakeside setting.

Putra Mosque

The iconic pink-domed mosque is Putrajaya's most recognizable landmark. Built with rose-tinted granite, this stunning mosque combines modern and traditional Islamic architecture, featuring a 116-meter minaret and beautiful lakefront views.

Key Features:

Pink Granite Dome 116m Minaret Lakefront Location Prayer Hall Islamic Architecture
